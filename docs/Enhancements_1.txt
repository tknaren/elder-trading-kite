A. Trade Bill
This screen consists of multiple sections
	1. Trade Info
		a. Ticker
		b. Current Market Price (CMP)
		c. Entry Price
		d. Stop Loss
		e. Target
		f. Quantity (fractional positions)
		g. Upper Channel
		h. Lower Channel
		i. Target Pips
		j. Stop Loss Pips
	2. Trade Risk
		a. Max Qty for Risk
		b. Overnight charges
		c. Risk per Share
		d. Position Size
		e. Risk %
	3. Reward Information
		a. Channel Height
		b. Potential Gain
		c. 1:1 C Target
		d. 1:2 B Target
		e. 1:3 A Target
		f. Potential Gain
		g. Risk (£)
		h. Reward (£)
		i. Risk Reward Ratio
	4. After the Entry
		a. Break Even
		b. Trailing Stop
	5. Actions (all checkboxes)
		a. Filled 
		b. Stop Entered
		c. Target Entered
		d. Journal Entered
	6. Comments (textbox)
	7. Save (store in database)
